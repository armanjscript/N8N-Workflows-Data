##Title
Financial incentives for energy efficiency in New Brunswick


##Description
If you own or operate a commercial building in New Brunswick, you could get up to $1.25 million to implement energy efficiency projects in order to reduce electricity consumption, increase competitiveness, and produce a healthier and more comfortable workspace. You could also receive $8,000 towards an audit to determine the potential for such upgrades.


##Money
Maximum: $1,250,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 100% of eligible costs.
