##Title
Funding for artificial intelligence in Northern Ontario


##Description
Access funding to boost artificial intelligence by bringing new technologies to market or by accelerating AI adoption in critical sectors, such as agriculture, health sciences, manufacturing, mining and forestry in Northern Ontario.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Funding limits
Maximum: 50% of capital costs 
Maximum: 75% of non-capital costs


##You
Incorporated small or medium-sized enterprise
Incorporated not-for-profit organization
Indigenous (First Nation/Métis/Inuit) community or organization



##Use this to
Undergo a project whose benefits must accrue to Northern Ontario
Drive innovation, growth, and productivity
>Pillar 1: AI Productization and Commercialization
.Technology and solution demonstration and/or commercialization 
.Business and capacity development 
>Pillar 2: AI Adoption
.Strategic adoption and integration 
.Process re-engineering and capacity development 


Government of Canada, Federal Economic Development Agency for Northern Ontario
