##Title
Funding to provide work skills to First Nations and Inuit youth


##Description
Get a contribution to develop projects to help First Nations and Inuit youth acquire essential employment skills and learn about job and career options.


##Status
Accepting project proposals
Closing on March 6, 2026



##Money
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 100% of eligible costs.


##How it works
1.Submit a detailed proposition.
2.Follow program guidelines.
3.Be gender-balanced.
4.Outline the activities that support the program's objectives.
5.Demonstrate that activities will provide assistance only to eligible participants.
6.Provide an estimate of eligible costs, including those assumed by partners.
7.Outline the results to be achieved by the project.


Government of Canada, Indigenous Services Canada
