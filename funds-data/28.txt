##Title
Financial support for book publisher business planning


##Description
Contributions of up to 75% of the total eligible expenses or $50,000 for business planning projects in Canadian book publishing companies owned and controlled by an Indigenous publisher, Official Language Minority, or firms owned and controlled by members of a Racialized community.


##Status
Accepting applications
Closing soon! Get your applications in by January 31, 2026


##Money
Maximum: $50,000
Non-repayable

##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 75% of eligible costs.
Stacking: you can combine money from this program with money from other government programs up to a total maximum of 100%.
