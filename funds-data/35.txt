##Title
Funding for workforce training in Quebec


##Description
Get a contribution to support the development of professional skills related to the job or task required of your employees, to make them more efficient and improves the retention of your staff.


##Status
Accepting clients


##You
Must show that the training:
Keeps people employed
Helps increase your employees’ performance
Is transferable to other members of your staff in order to increase mobility within your business.


##How it works
1.Contact a business advisor from the Services Québec office in your region.
2.Support will be offered to analyze your needs. 
3.Solutions adapted to your situation will then be proposed.


Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'emploi et de la solidarité sociale
