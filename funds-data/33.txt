##Title
Get financial aid to install electric charging stations at your Quebec business


##Description
Reimbursement for Quebec businesses, municipalities and public bodies of up to $49,000 per eligible building per fiscal year for the purchase and installation of electric vehicle charging stations at your workplace for employees.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $49,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 50% of eligible costs.


Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'Environnement, de la Lutte contre les changements climatiques, de la Faune et des Parcs
