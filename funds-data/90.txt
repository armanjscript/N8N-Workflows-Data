##Title
Money to enhance export marketing for Prince Edward Island businesses


##Description
Get up to $15,000 to cover 45% of the costs of short-term projects relating to export strategy, planning and marketing activities.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $15,000


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 45% of eligible costs


##How it works
Funding is provided in the form of a reimbursement. The applicant must fund 100% of the costs and will be reimbursed once the project is completed.
