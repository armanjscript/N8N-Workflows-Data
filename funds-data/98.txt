##Title
Funding for micro-businesses in the N.W.T.


##Description
Micro businesses can get funding of up to $6,000 over three years for the purchase of tools, equipment, traditional harvesting activities, and other self-employment activities.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $6,000 over three years



##Use this to 
>Purchase:
.Tools and equipment to increase the quality or quantity of production related to arts and crafts or the traditional economy
.Raw materials for the production of traditional arts and crafts
.Film making equipment
.Prospecting supply, equipment and other expenses directly related to prospecting activity
>Cover other self-employment activities



Government of the Northwest Territories, Department of Industry, Tourism and Investment
