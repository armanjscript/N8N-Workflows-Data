##Title
Financial support for Manitoba First Nation businesses


##Description
Get a non-repayable contribution of up to 40% of eligible costs for start-up, expansion or acquisition of a business that is majority Manitoba First Nation-owned.



##Status
Accepting applications



##Money
Maximum: $250,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 40% of eligible costs.
Stacking: you can combine money from this program with money from other government programs up to a total maximum of 50%.



First Peoples Economic Growth Fund Inc.
