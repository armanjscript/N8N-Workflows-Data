##Title
Funding for R&D projects in Saskatchewan


##Description
Receive up to a maximum of $450,000 in grants to support the commercialization of innovation or technology in the following sectors: Mining, Energy, Manufacturing and Processing, and Healthcare.



##Money
Maximum: $450,000
Non-repayable 



##Funding limits
This program will cover a maximum of 30% of the project's budget
Industry partners must provide 50% or more of the budget
In-kind matching contributions cannot exceed 20% of the budget



##Your project
Supports industry-driven R&D projects that address an industry challenge in the province's core economic sectors
Accelerates the commercialization and adoption of innovative industrial technologies
Drives economic growth through technological innovations



Government of Saskatchewan, Innovation Saskatchewan
