##Title
Money to help Atlantic Canada businesses export


##Description
Get up to $15,000 to cover 65% of the costs related to short-term projects aimed at developing and adopting electronic tools that can help you get your products and services to international markets more efficiently.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Minimum: $3,250
Maximum: $15,000
Non-repayable



##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 65% of eligible costs.
