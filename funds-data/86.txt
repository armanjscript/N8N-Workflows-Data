##Title
Money for New Brunswick agrifood businesses


##Description
Get up to $15,000 to develop agrifood, fish, and aquaculture products.


##Status
Accepting clients



##Money
Maximum: $15,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 50% of eligible costs.


Government of New Brunswick, Department of Agriculture, Aquaculture and Fisheries
