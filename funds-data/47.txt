##Title
Integration into defence supply chains


##Description
Funding to accelerate business and regional ecosystem integration into defence supply chains and boost industrial and innovation capacity to meet defence certification and qualification standards to respond to calls for proposals from prime defence contractors and the Government of Canada.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Funding Limits
SMEs, contributions are repayable. Financial assistance may cover up to 75% of eligible costs
Non-profit organizations, contributions are generally non-repayable. Financial assistance may cover up to 90% of eligible costs



##Eligibility
SMEs active or looking to position themselves within the defence industry supply chain
Not-for-profit organizations that support admissible SMEs
Indigenous recipients


##Your project
You must demonstrate how your project meets current or future needs of the Canadian Armed Forces, NATO or an allied country


Government of Canada, Canada Economic Development for Quebec Region
