##Title
Complete analysis of greenhouse gas reduction options for Quebec companies


##Description
Up to $300,000 to cover 75% of the costs of conducting a complete analysis of opportunities to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.


##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
Maximum: $300,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 75% of eligible costs.
Stacking: you can combine money from this program with money from other government programs up to a total maximum of 75%.


Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'Environnement, de la Lutte contre les changements climatiques, de la Faune et des Parcs
