##Title
Money for quantum technologies and solutions in Quebec


##Description
Funding focused on adopting, developing and commercializing quantum technologies for domestic and global markets.


##Status
Accepting project proposals


##Funding Limits
For SMEs: Contributions are generally refundable or conditionally refundable
For NPOs: Contributions are generally non-refundable


##Use this to 
Enhance the commercialization of quantum innovations
Integrate quantum technologies into business operations
Promote sustainable and inclusive practices
