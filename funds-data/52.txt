##Title
Funding for targeted RCMs to strengthen their economic vitality


##Description
Get a non-repayable contribution for your SME to stimulate the emergence, growth and expansion of entrepreneurs located in the territories of participating RCMs.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
>Minimum: $15,000
>Maximum: $150,000
>This includes a:
.Up to $25,000 per year for the hiring of a technical or undergraduate professional or equivalent in the area of competency required by the job.
.Maximum of $35,000 per year to hire a university graduate professional (master’s or doctorate) in the area of expertise required for project deployment
.Maximum of $300,000 per business


##Funding limits
This program will cover a maximum of 50% of eligible salary expenses
May not exceed 40% of salary, including benefits, for a maximum period of 1 year.
Stacking: For a feasibility study, you can combine the amount from this program with other government programs up to 80%. For other projects, you can combine the amount from this program with other government programs up to 70%


##You
Have a for-profit business with 250 employees or fewer
Are legally incorporated under the laws of the Government of Quebec or Canada
You are established and active in Quebec
Be registered in Quebec
Other conditions may apply


**Note: The territories covered are:
.RCMs in the last quintile of the economic vitality index ranking
.The RCMs of the administrative region of Gaspésie–Îles-de-la-Madeleine, including the maritime community of the Magdalen Islands
.Appalachian RCM
.The MRC du Granit, which includes the city of Lac-Mégantic
.Indigenous communities located within the territorial boundaries of eligible RCMs and that originate from one of the nations recognized by the National Assembly of Quebec

Gouvernement du Québec, Investissement Québec
