##Title
Support for genomics commercialization and engineering biology in Ontario



##Description
Access funding, mentorship, infrastructure, investor showcase, and business support to enable your SME to commercialize genomics and engineering biology enabled products and/or technologies in the health, food and agriculture, and cleantech sectors.



##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Up to $150,000 with a minimal contribution of $100,000



##You project
Is at a Technology Readiness Level (TRL) of 4+
Must be led by an SME located in southern Ontario
Must be performed in Ontario and awarded funds cannot flow outside of Ontario unless approved
Should be 18-months in duration
