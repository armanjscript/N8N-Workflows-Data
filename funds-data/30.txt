##Title
Funding for projects in the book industry


##Description
Contributions of up to $2 million for marketing, technology and professional development projects in the book publishing, distribution, marketing, wholesale or retail sectors representing Canadian authors.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $2,000,000
Non-repayable



##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 75% of eligible costs.
Stacking: you can combine money from this program with money from other government programs up to a total maximum of 100%.


##How it works
If your project starts between September 1 and March 31, the deadline for your application is April 30 at 4:00 p.m. (ET).
If your project starts between April 1 and August 31, the deadline for your application is October 31 at 4:00 p.m. (ET).
When an application deadline falls on a Saturday, Sunday or statutory holiday, it is extended to the following working day.
