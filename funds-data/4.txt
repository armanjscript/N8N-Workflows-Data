##Title
Money to support business growth in Quebec


##Description
Your organization could get a financial contribution to assist businesses at all stages of development in scaling up and becoming more globally competitive. Investments will focus on enhancing business support networks for under-represented groups including women, Indigenous peoples and young entrepreneurs and supporting regional strategic clusters, accelerators and incubators, and projects that attract foreign investments.


##Status
Accepting project proposals


##Money
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 90% of eligible costs, and a maximum of 50% for capital projects for not-for-profit organizations


##You
Are a non-profit organization that provides services for businesses
Are a business incubator or accelerator
Are a municipality or regional county municipality
Are a group or association
Are an Indigenous organization


##Your project
Fosters closer ties among players in the various ecosystems
Fosters the creation, growth and maintenance of regional clusters
Attracts foreign investment


##How it works
1.Read the project submission guide, including information about programs, your regional intervention strategy and the eligibility criteria.
2.Contact an advisor from the business office for your region if you have any questions.
3.Prepare your financial assistance application for the project.
4.Submit your financial assistance application.
5.You will be sent a letter confirming that your file is being reviewed and that you can expect a decision within 35 to 65 days.
