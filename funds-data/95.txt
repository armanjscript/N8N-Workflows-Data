##Title
Contributions for organizations and municipalities in Nunavut


##Description
Access contributions for your project of economic development nature presenting measurable tangible development benefits such as jobs, training opportunities, etc.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Use this to
Help build community economies and support regional economic development in key sectors of the Nunavut economy
Organize events
Offer professional development resources
Provide education, training and physical community assets


Government of Nunavut, Department of Economic Development and Transportation
