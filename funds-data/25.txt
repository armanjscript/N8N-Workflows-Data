##Title
Funding to implement energy efficiency measures in Quebec


##Description
Financial assistance for businesses that have one or more buildings used for commercial, institutional or industrial activities to implement energy efficiency measures involving lighting, variable-frequency drives, compressed air, water chilling and industrial refrigeration.


##Your project
Will be implemented in at least one eligible building
Is targeting measures designed to improve the energy efficiency of a building, a plant, a piece of equipment, a process or a system
Must meet the eligibility criteria


##Use this to
Make your heating more efficient
Optimize the power demand of your equipment
Limit losses related to lighting


Gouvernement du Québec, Hydro-Québec
