##Title
Money to hire tech interns in book publishing


##Description
Contribution of up to 50% of the total eligible expenses for internships in a technology role with a Canadian book publisher or $30,000 or 75% of eligible costs up to $45,000 in the case of Indigenous interns, interns from a Racialized community, interns from official language minority communities or interns with a print disability,



##Status
Accepting applications
Closing soon! Get your applications in by January 31, 2026


##Money
Maximum: $45,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 75% of eligible costs.
