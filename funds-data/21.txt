##Title
Financial assistance for energy management systems in Quebec


##Description
Up to $320,000 to cover 75% of the costs of implementing an energy management system in order to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.


##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
Maximum: $10,000 to hire an energy manager
Maximum: $50,000 for energy management training and ISO 50001
Maximum: $100,000 for coaching and consulting of external specialists for system implementation, third party audits and certification by an accredited certification body
Maximum: $150,000 for measurement equipment, probes and programming
Maximum: $10,000 for ISO 50001 certification by accredited certification body


##Funding limits
This program will cover a maximum of 75% of eligible costs


Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'Environnement, de la Lutte contre les changements climatiques, de la Faune et des Parcs
