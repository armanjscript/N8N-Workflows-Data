##Title
Grants for businesses that employ reservists


##Description
If you are a civilian employer of reservists or a self-employed reservist, get financial assistance to offset operational costs you incur when reservists deploy on full-time operational duty, both domestically and abroad.


##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
Non-repayable
Funding covers 40% of the Yearly Maximum Pensionable Earnings divided by 52 weeks, multiplied by the number of weeks the reservist is deployed


##You
Employ a reservist who is a member of the Primary Reserve or Canadian Rangers deployed for a minimum of 30 days on a Chief of Defence Staff directed operation and worked for you for a minimum of 30 hours/week for 3 consecutive months prior to the leave
Are a self-employed reservist deployed for a minimum of 30 days on a Chief of Defence Staff directed operation
