##Title
Money for aspiring farmers in Quebec


##Description
Subsidy, depending on education and training, to young people under the age of 40 who wish to start their own agricultural business or join an existing one.


##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
>Full-time establishment grant
.Maximum: $50,000
.Minimum: $20,000
>Part-time start grant
.Maximum: $25,000
.Minimum: $10,000


##You
Are domiciled in Québec
Are at least 18 years of age but not yet 40
Have some recognized training
Hold at least 20% of the operation's shares
Count at least one year's relevant experience in agriculture
Have a potentially profitable start-up or establishment project


##Use this to
Improve land
Purchase equipment
Acquire agricultural, administration, or management training


Gouvernement du Québec, La Financière agricole
