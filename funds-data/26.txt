##Title
Money for energy management systems in Quebec


##Description
If the annual electricity bill for your business exceeds $750,000, you may be able to get up to $175,000 to cover up to 50% of the costs to purchase and install diagnostic equipment, or to implement an elictricity management system (EMS) or an energy management information system (EMIS).


##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
Maximum: $175,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 50% of eligible costs


##Use this to
Develop, implement or improve an electrical energy management system (EMS)
Measure in real time consumption related to their main processes, systems and equipment
Have the necessary resources to carry out the resulting activities


##How it works
Consult the Participant Workbook.
Send your project information.
Complete the Project outline form (Document d’avant-projet (DAP) in French only).


Gouvernement du Québec, Hydro-Québec
