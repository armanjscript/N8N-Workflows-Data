##Title
Money for study and action projects in the St. Lawrence ecosystem



##Description
Get up to $200,000 in funding for a study and action project pertaining to a specific environmental issue with the aim of identifying concrete actions to be taken in the fields.


##Status
Accepting clients



##Money
Maximum: $200,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 70% of eligible costs.


##How it works
1.Every year, you must submit your funding application to the CIP by one of the following submission deadlines: March 1 or October 15.
2.To be complete, your project proposal must contain all items requested in the CIP funding application form.
