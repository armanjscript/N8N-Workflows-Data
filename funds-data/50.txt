##Title
Support for large Canadian enterprises


##Description
Get support in the form of short-term financing to cover a twelve-month liquidity shortfall if your large company is affected by new tariffs and countermeasures and faces challenges accessing traditional sources of market financing.


##Status
Accepting clients


##You
Have an impact on Canada’s economy as a result of:
Significant operations in Canada
A significant workforce in Canada.
Have approximately $150 million, or more, in annual Canadian revenue
Require a minimum loan size of $30 million
Must commit to minimizing loss of employment, sustaining your domestic business activities and must demonstrate that funding under the LETL facility forms part of your overall transition plan to return to financial stability


##How it works
The loan size for each applicant will be assessed on a case-by-case basis based on demonstrated need, the reasonableness of management’s business plan assumptions and the ability to repay.


Government of Canada, Canada Development Investment Corporation
