##Title
Assistance for Francophone projects in southern Ontario


##Description
Access funding to support projects encouraging economic diversification, business development, innovation, partnerships and to increase support for small- and medium-sized enterprises in Francophone communities of Southern Ontario.



##Status
Accepting applications



##Money
Maximum: Up to $500,000 per year



##Funding limits
Maximum: Up to 70% of eligible project costs



##You
Have an incorporated Francophone or bilingual not-for-profit organization located in southern Ontario that provides programs or services in French to Francophone communities


##Use this to 
Support economic development and diversification projects that respond to the needs of Francophone communities
Support business and economic development projects that respond to the needs and foster the economic growth of industry sectors such as manufacturing, information technology, and more
Support strategic community economic planning projects that enhance the economic base and competitiveness of Francophone communities and small- and medium-sized enterprises



**Note: Projects must focus on incremental activities and must increase the services and/or products of an organization above and beyond the organization's current day-to-day operations or already funded activities.


Government of Canada, Federal Economic Development Agency for Southern Ontario
