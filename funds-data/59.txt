##Title
Funding for supply-managed sectors facilities improvements


##Description
Receive cost-sharing funding for your for-profit organization in the supply-managed sectors to invest in your processing facilities to improve productivity and/or efficiency through the acquisition of new automated equipment and technology.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $10 million for dairy processors
Maximum: $5 million to all other eligible applicants listed


##You
Have a for-profit organization, operating as a/an:
Dairy processor
Poultry Primary processor (chicken and turkey)
Poultry Further processor (chicken and turkey)
Hatcherie (broiler, egg-type or turkey)
Egg grader
Egg processor


##Use this to
Automate an existing production process
Improve an existing automated or robotic process
Purchase a new production line
Implement or improve an integrated management software


Government of Canada, Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada
