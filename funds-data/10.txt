##Title
Financing to accelerate growth in Quebec


##Description
Get a financial assistance to help you scale up and expand, adopt and commercialize innovative technologies and processes, or diversify your markets.



##Status
Accepting project proposals


##Funding limits
This program will cover up to 50% of authorized costs
This funding is repayable, without interest
Repayment begins two years after the end of the project


**Examples of funded projects 
Acquisition of digital production equipment or machinery and infrastructure
Commercialization and market development
Business management

Other activities may also be eligible


Government of Canada, Canada Economic Development for Quebec Regions
