##Title
Funding for accelerated adoption of AI in Quebec


##Description
Your SME could receive funding for the development and commercialization of new technologies and the adoption of AI in industries such as agriculture, clean technology, life sciences, and manufacturing, and support transformative projects.


##Status
Accepting project proposals


##Funding limits
>For SMEs
.up to 50% of authorized costs
.repayable and interest-free 
>For NPOs
.up to 90% of authorized costs
.Non-repayable financial assistance


##Your project
>Must contribute to one of the two pillars:
.To support the development of products and the commercialization of AI by start-ups and growing businesses
.To accelerate the adoption or integration of AI by SMEs, including in critical sectors 


Note: Visit the program website to get all the details
