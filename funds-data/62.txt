##Title
Access to investment capital


##Description
Investment for start-up of young innovative businesses with growth potential located in Quebec region.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Minimum: $250 000
Maximum: $1 million ($750 000 for the Montreal region)
Maximum: $2 million for the biopharmaceutical sector


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 50% of eligible costs


##You
Are a not-for-profit organization
Are a young for-profit business
Show strong development potential
Operate in Quebec


##How it works
>Documents to be submitted:
1.A request for investment.
2.An accompanying plan signed by an accompanying entity.
3.One or more letters of intent from a recognized investor or private investors.
4.A letter of recommendation signed by a recognized referral entity.
5.A due diligence form completed by the recognized investor.
