##Title
Strategic support for projects critical to economic resilience and growth


##Description
Support for large-scale projects in highly trade-exposed sectors or companies experiencing major financial challenges involving front-end development and capital investment.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Minimum project costs: $20 million
Minimum contribution: $10 million


##Eligibility
Not-for-profit organization incorporated in Canada
For-profit corporation incorporated in Canada
Carrying out business in Canada
Companies that have been impacted by U.S. and global tariffs may qualify


Note: For more eligibility information, please reach out to the program at strategicresponsefund-fondsdereponsestrategique@ised-isde.gc.ca.


##Use this to
Support domestic competitiveness and Canadian innovation 
Grow and transform industrial capabilities
Develop innovative products and technologies
Pivot to new markets
