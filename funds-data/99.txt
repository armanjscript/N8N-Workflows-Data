##Title
Funding for art activities in the Yukon


##Description
Access funds to cover up to 70% of eligible project costs for group projects that develop the arts and engage the public in the Yukon.



##Status
Accepting applications


##Funding limits
70% of eligible project expenses


##Use this to
Develop a festival and special initiatives
Implement training activities
Perform, produce events and more


Government of Yukon, Department of Tourism and Culture
