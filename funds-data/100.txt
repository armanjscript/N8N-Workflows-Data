##Title
Grant and assistance for Ontario small businesses


##Description
Access training, mentoring and a grant of up to $5,000 for starting, expanding or buying a small business in Ontario. Depending on location, the program opens and closes at various times of the year. To enquire, visit the link and contact your local Small Business Enterprise Centre.


##Money
Maximum: $5,000


**Note: To qualify for a grant, you must be able to make a contribution in cash or in kind. It has to be at least 25% of the grant amount



##You
Are 18 years of age or older
Are an Ontario resident and Canadian citizen (or permanent resident)
Are not attending school full-time or returning to school


##Use this to
>Get one-on-one guidance 
>Participate in business workshops, seminars or networking events
>Learn about topics ranging from:
.Writing a business plan
.Budgeting
.Marketing campaigns
>Talk to a mentor with experience in starting, expanding or buying a small business
