##Title
Funds to decarbonize building heating in Quebec


##Description
Obtain funding for the partial conversion of buildings in the commercial and institution sectors using natural gas for space heating with electricity, through an approach based on optimal complementarity of electricity and gas networks.


##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
Simplified path - Maximum: $250,000 per site
Tailor-made route - Maximum: $6 million per site


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 80% of eligible costs


Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'Environnement, de la Lutte contre les changements climatiques, de la Faune et des Parcs
