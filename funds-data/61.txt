##Title
Funding to optimize the operation of building mechanical systems


##Description
Receive 75% of eligible costs up to $100,000 for the completion of analyses, audits, and procedures for the recommissioning of building heating, ventilation and air conditioning systems, regardless of the form of energy used.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $100,000


##Funding limits
This program will cover a maximum of 75% of the eligible costs


##Use this to
Cover fees of the accredited agent and consultants
Pay internal specialist fees
Cover rental costs for measuring equipment


Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'Environnement, de la Lutte contre les changements climatiques, de la Faune et des Parcs
