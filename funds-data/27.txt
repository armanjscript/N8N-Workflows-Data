##Title
Financial assistance for the demonstration of new technologies in Quebec


##Description
Get a contribution to cover up to 50% of the cost of energy and greenhouse gas (GHG) emission reduction technology demonstration projects, either for the development of a pre-commercial innovation or for the testing of a technology that is not yet available on the Quebec market.


##Status
Accepting statements of interest


##Money
Maximum: $3,000,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 50% of eligible costs.
Stacking: you can combine money from this program with money from other government programs up to a total maximum of 75%.


Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'Environnement, de la Lutte contre les changements climatiques, de la Faune et des Parcs
