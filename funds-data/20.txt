##Title
Financial assistance for greenhouse gas reduction


##Description
Up to $5 million per project and maximum of $10 million per year per site for projects that reduce greenhouse gas emissions.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $10,000,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 75% of eligible costs.


Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'Environnement, de la Lutte contre les changements climatiques, de la Faune et des Parcs
