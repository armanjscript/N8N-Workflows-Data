##Title
Funding to grow your business in the NWT


##Description
Access up to $75,000 to make large investments in your company in order to expand and grow your activities.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $75,000


##Your project 
Is consistent with Department strategies and/or regional economic development plans
Leverages funding sources outside the Government of Northwest Territories



##Use this to
Increase the business and economic activity of the local community, or communities
Increase employment outcomes 



Government of the Northwest Territories, Department of Industry, Tourism and Investment
