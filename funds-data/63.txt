##Title
Funding to enhance supply chains with AI


##Description
Get funding to cover up to 50% of the costs of collaborative projects that accelerate the adoption or commercialization of artificial intelligence (AI) for supply chains, when your SME partners with other Scale AI members.



##Status
Accepting project proposals


##Your project
Must align with Scale AI’s strategy
Must be new, not funded by another government entity
Can demonstrate that Scale AI Funding would help bring it to life
Include more than one participating entity
Involve at least one SME
Have the endorsement of the applicant’s CEO or other executive 
Must be ready to launch shortly after an investment decision is made by Scale AI
Work must be executed in Canada



##How it works
1.Submit your initial project proposal to Scale AI, who will assess its eligibility.
2.Work with the Scale AI team to prepare and submit a detailed proposal.
3.To receive funding Non-Scale AI members must become members.
