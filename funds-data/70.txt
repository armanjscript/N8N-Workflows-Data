##Title
Get funding to assist your Nova Scotia-based seafood company


##Description
Receive up to $15,000 to cover 50% of your project to develop new value-added products, overcome technical challenges with existing products, update food safety programs, and gain access to new markets.



##Status
Accepting applications
Closing on July 30, 2026



##Use this to
Ensure that current seafood products meet customer expectation
Develop and implement comprehensive food safety programs
Develop new value-added products


For eligibility criteria and more information on the application process, please view the Program Guidelines


Government of Nova Scotia, Department of Fisheries and Aquaculture
