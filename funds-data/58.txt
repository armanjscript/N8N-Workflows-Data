##Title
Funding for business innovation and growth projects


##Description
Receive contributions for activities related to business innovation and growth projects such as R&D and commercialization, firm expansion and growth, and investment attraction and reinvestment - Applications are accepted through the Strategic Response Fund.


##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
Minimum project costs: $20 million
Minimum funding: $10 million


##You
Have a for-profit business or cooperative incorporated in Canada
Are part of a partnership proposing to carry out business in Canada


##Use this to
Undergo R&D to test the commercial potential of early TRL concepts or findings
Adapt research findings for commercial applications that have the potential to disrupt the market
Increase manufacturing capacity to meet demonstrated market demand
Establish a new production facility that did not previously exist in Canada
Other

Note: For more information on the Strategic Response Fund and implications on the Strategic Innovation Fund, please refer to the frequently asked questions Strategic Response Fund or contact strategicresponsefund-fondsdereponsestrategique@ised-isde.gc.ca.
