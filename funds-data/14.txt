##Title
Funding for pre-commercial agriculture and agri-food science


##Description
Your not-for-profit organization can get up to $5 million per project and $10 million per applicant over 5 years to fund 70% of the cost of pre-commercial scientific research in priority areas.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $10,000,000
Non-repayable- More Information


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 70% of eligible costs.


Government of Canada, Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada
