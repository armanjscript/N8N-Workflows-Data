##Title
Financial support for large-scale industrial projects


##Description
Financial assistance of up to $40 million for the implementation of major projects to reduce GHG emissions at industrial sites.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $40 million


##Funding limits
This program will cover a maximum of 75% of eligible costs


##Use this to
Start an energy efficiency project
Carry out energy conversion projects
Initiate fugitive emissions reduction projects


##How it works
>The financial assistance granted corresponds, per measure, to the lesser of the amounts calculated for the following parameters:
1.The amount necessary to reduce the measure's unassisted investment payback period (PRIsa) to the minimum investment payback period (PRImin).
2.The maximum of a percentage of eligible expenditures (%Iadm) per measure.
3.The maximum dollar amount based on the number of tons of GHG emissions reduced annually per measure, applied at a rate of $100/t for a ten-year commitment period.
4.The amount requested by the participant.
