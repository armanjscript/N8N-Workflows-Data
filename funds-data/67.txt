##Title
Support for hydrocarbon recovery technology


##Description
Get funding for projects that improve hydrocarbon recovery while reducing associated emissions, lowering costs, and enabling new resources to be developed.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $ 2 million


##Your Project
Improves recovery efficiency
Creates value for operators
Reduces associated emissions
Unlocks new resources



##How it works
1.Contact a Clean Resources Project Advisor to determine if the project scope meets the requirements.
2.Register on SmartSimple, the online application portal.
3.Complete and submit the Intake Form on SmartSimple.
4.Wait for your Intake Form to be reviewed to determine if the proposed project will be invited to submit a detailed proposal.
