##Title
Funding for seminars and tradeshows for NWT businesses


##Description
Get up to $8,000 per year to attend conferences, tradeshows, and seminars to access information on new technologies and business opportunities to build your business.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $8,000 per year
Maximum: $4,000 per resident per trip


##You
NWT resident
NWT business


##Use this to
Build your business
Connect with the information and contacts you need 
Attend seminars and trade shows
Access information regarding new technologies or business opportunities


Government of the Northwest Territories, Department of Industry, Tourism and Investment
