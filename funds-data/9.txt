##Title
Funding for innovations the government needs


##Description
Your small business could get funding to come up with an innovative solution to a government challenge, with access to early-stage development, testing and validation of a prototype, and a pathway to commercialization.


##You 
Are a for-profit entrepreneur
Are incorporated in Canada (federally or provincially)
Have fewer than 499 full-time employees
Do research and development activities that take place in Canada


##How it works
1.Read the challenge requirements.
2.Begin the online application process by clicking the “propose a solution” button (Note: You’ll be asked to share the details of your proposal, along with some questions to further assess your eligibility and financial requirements.) 
3.If your proposal is selected, the sponsor will reach out to you with more information on the next steps in the grant award process. 
4.Begin the project and report as needed.
