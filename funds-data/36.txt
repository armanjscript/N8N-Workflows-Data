##Title
Subsidy to hire people with disabilities in Quebec


##Description
Get salary support and reimbursement of certain additional expenses necessary for the integration and employment of a person with a disability.


##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
Non-repayable


##Use this to
>Fill a vacant or newly created, temporary or permanent position in a standard work environment. The work schedule may be:
.full-time (maximum of 40 hours per week);
.part-time, depending on the potential of the disabled person and the needs of the employer (minimum of 12 hours per week)

Gouvernement du Québec, Ministère de l'emploi et de la solidarité sociale
