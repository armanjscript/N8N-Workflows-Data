##Title
Funding for the awareness of citizens to the quality of the St. Lawrence ecosystem


##Description
Get up to $100,000 in funding for an awareness project arising from a specific environmental issue that target a particular group in the community and lead to concrete short-term actions.


##Status
Accepting applications


##Money
Maximum: $100,000
Non-repayable


##Funding Limits
This program will cover a maximum of 70% of eligible costs.


##How it works
1.Every year, you must submit your funding application to the CIP by one of the following submission deadlines: March 1 or October 15.
2.To be complete, your project proposal must contain all items requested in the CIP funding application form.
