##Title
Funding to create summer jobs for First Nations and Inuit students


##Description
Get a contribution when you develop a project that creates summer jobs of at least 80 hours for First Nations and Inuit youth.


##Status
Accepting project proposals
Closing soon! Get your project proposals in by March 6, 2026


##Funding limits
Not-for-profits - Maximum 100% of applicable wages + mandatory employment- related costs
Private sector - Maximum 50% of applicable wages + mandatory employment- related costs
All employers are eligible to apply for 100% of the wage when a participant with disabilities is hired
A maximum contribution of $3,000 per participant may be available, on an actual cost basis, for special equipment and facilities to accommodate the student's needs



##How it works
1.Submit a detailed proposition.
2.Follow program guidelines.
3.Be gender-balanced.
4.Outline the activities that support the program's objectives.
5.Demonstrate that activities will provide assistance only to eligible participants.
6.Provide an estimate of eligible costs, including those assumed by partners.
7.Outline the results to be achieved by the project.
