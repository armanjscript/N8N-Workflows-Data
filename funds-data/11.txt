##Title
Funds for First Nations entrepreneurs in Quebec


##Description
Get a non-refundable contribution of up to $99,999 for business start-up or expansion, marketing, project management, professional advisory services, and development of a business plan, marketing plan or feasibility studies.



##Status
Accepting clients


##Money
Maximum: $99,999


##You
Are part of a First Nation or the Native Alliance of Quebec.
Are a member of a First Nation or the Native Alliance of Quebec.
Are a corporation owned by a First Nation, the Native Alliance of Quebec or by one of their members.
Have the necessary financial resources (equity: 10% minimum of the cost of the project)
Have a good credit rating.
Have experience and training in the field of activities.
Operate a business not involved in the following sectors of activity (gambling, sex, alcohol, tobacco).
